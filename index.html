Prostate Pathology (Patologia da Próstata)

Introduction

This explorer provides an overview of prostate pathology, including common diseases, symptoms, and treatment options.

Common Diseases

  • Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH)
  • Prostate Cancer
  • Prostatitis

Symptoms

Common symptoms of prostate disorders include:

  • Difficulty urinating
  • Frequent urination, especially at night
  • Painful urination
  • Blood in urine or semen

Treatment Options

Treatment may vary depending on the specific prostate condition. Common treatment options include:

  • Medications
  • Surgery
  • Radiation therapy
  • Hormonal therapy
